At Infinityfarecompare, we understand that plans can change. Whether it's a shift in schedule or a change of heart, we aim to make the cancellation process as straightforward as possible. The following policy outlines how cancellations are handled for different types of bookings made through our platform.

General Guidelines

All cancellation requests must be made in writing and sent to {{getData?.email}}. The request should include your booking reference number and the reason for cancellation. Once received, we will begin the review process based on the type of service booked and the terms of the respective provider.

Service-Specific Cancellations

Each travel service has its own cancellation rules. Airlines, hotel chains, and cruise operators may impose different deadlines, refund eligibility criteria, or cancellation charges. Please refer to the cancellation conditions provided during the booking process, as those will determine the final outcome of your request.

Refunds and Deductions

If your cancellation qualifies for a refund, the approved amount will be returned to your original payment method after internal verification. Refunds may be subject to deductions such as service fees, transaction charges, or penalties imposed by the travel provider. Some discounted or promotional bookings may be non-refundable as per provider terms.

Key Points to Note

  • Refund timelines are governed by third-party providers and may vary.
  • Infinityfarecompare acts as an intermediary platform and cannot guarantee refund processing times set by airlines, hotels, or cruise operators.
  • We strongly advise reviewing all cancellation terms carefully before confirming your booking.
