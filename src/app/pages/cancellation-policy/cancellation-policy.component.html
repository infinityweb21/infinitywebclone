At Theinfinitytravel, we understand that plans can change. Whether it's a shift in schedule or a change of heart, we aim to make the cancellation process as straightforward as possible. The following policy outlines how cancellations are handled for different types of bookings made through our platform.

General Guidelines

All cancellation requests must be made in writing and sent to {{"{{getData?.email}}"}}. The request should include your booking reference number and the reason for cancellation. Once received, we will begin the review process based on the type of service booked and the terms of the respective provider.

Service-Specific Policies

Cancellation terms may vary depending on the service booked, such as flights, accommodations, cruises, or activities. Each service provider has its own rules regarding eligibility for refunds, applicable charges, and deadlines. Please refer to the individual terms shared at the time of booking.

Refund Process

If a cancellation qualifies for a refund, the amount will be processed back to the original payment method after verification. Please note that service fees, transaction charges, and provider penalties may be deducted from the total refundable amount. Some bookings may be non-refundable, especially those made during promotional periods or with strict rate plans.

Important Considerations

Delays in processing may occur due to provider timelines or unforeseen circumstances. Theinfinitytravel serves as a booking platform and does not influence third-party refund timelines. We suggest going through all booking details and conditions thoroughly before completing your reservation.

For any clarification or to submit a cancellation request, please reach out to us at {{"{{getData?.email}}"}}. Our goal is to ensure your travel experience is as flexible as possible, even when plans change.
