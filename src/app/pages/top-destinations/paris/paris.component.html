Paris

The Timeless Heart of France

The city is a stunning blend of history and modern flair located along the banks of the Seine River graced by iconic silhouettes like the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame and the Sacré-Cœur. Each neighborhood has its own charm from the bohemian of Montmartre to the Saint-Germain-des-Prés with quiet moments in a riverside café and the grandeur of opera at Palais Garnier.

The way of life here is slow and you can start your day with a flaky croissant in a corner bakery, wander through the halls of the Louvre, enjoy an afternoon walk in the Tuileries Garden and end the night with wine under the twinkling lights of the Eiffel Tower. The city delivers a full experience with grace and passion whether it’s art, rich history or exclusive food. Every street feels like a postcard and every moment like a memory as the city awaits with open arms and a touch of elegance for any season.

Eiffel Tower

No visit to this city is complete without witnessing its most iconic structure with marvelous views from the top whether you take the elevator or climb the stairs. Stay until evening when the tower lights up in a glittering spectacle every hour.

Louvre Museum

It is a journey through art and home to various paintings including the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Mil. Its glass pyramid entrance has become a modern icon and the museum itself is worth hours of exploration.

Montmartre

This hilltop neighborhood offers cobbled streets, street art and panoramic views of the city. Visit the stunning Sacré-Cœur Basilica, watch artists paint in Place du Tertre and enjoy the bohemian atmosphere that once inspired legends like Picasso and Toulouse-Lautrec.

Seine River Cruise

See the city from a different angle with a leisurely cruise along the river and glide under historic bridges, past grand monuments and enjoy commentary that brings the city’s past to life with magical experience at night.

Palace of Versailles

This former royal residence is a symbol of absolute monarchy at its most lavish and it is just a short trip from the city. Wander through the Hall of Mirrors, walk through the gardens and imagine life in the grandeur of the French court.

Book Now

Latin Quarter

This historic neighborhood near the Sorbonne University is perfect for discovering hidden gems with full of bookstores, jazz clubs and old-world charm. Enjoy a crêpe on Rue Mouffetard or browse ancient texts at Shakespeare and Company.
