LET’S DO LONDON NOW!

Are you dreaming of eloping from the busy schedule of your life? Get away into the fascinating places across London. Since the beginning of time, it has always been one of the best places for everyone to escape from the everyday grind.

Here’s what you need to know before visiting London:

  • Experience the royalty when you step into the streets of London.
  • Smell the fresh air of independence that prevails throughout the cities and town halls.
  • Embark on an unforgettable adventure that will stay with you for a lifetime.
  • Make new friendships amid diverse cultures that will rekindle memories long after your trip.
  • Enjoy entertainment in the hidden gems of London with your loved ones.

What to Expect in London, as You Arrive?

From the moment you arrive, prepare for a warm welcome. Londoners are known for their friendly nature. Whether you’re lost or in need of help, you’ll likely find someone willing to assist you. And don’t miss out on tasting delicious dishes from some of the world’s top chefs!

What to Do in London After Covid?

Here are some must-do activities in London post-Covid:

  • Get creative by drawing on the walls of the Turbine Hall.
  • Enjoy a magical West End musical show.
  • Play interactive street games at the Southbank Centre’s chalk installation zone.
  • Meet celebrity wax figures at Madame Tussauds.
  • Greet the Beefeaters and explore the history of the Tower of London.
  • Step inside the Butterfly House at the Horniman Museum & Gardens.
  • Let kids roleplay careers at Kidzania in a fun learning experience.
  • Travel into space at the Peter Harrison Planetarium at Royal Observatory Greenwich.
  • Challenge your family at The Crystal Maze LIVE Experience London.
  • Watch daily pelican feedings and spot wildlife in St James’s Park.
