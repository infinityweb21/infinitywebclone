Amsterdam

Amsterdam: The Soul of the Netherlands

The capital of the Dutch is located along a vast network of canals with history and modern life blend effortlessly. As you walk through cobblestone streets framed by charming 17th-century houses, the city opens up with vibrant neighborhoods, world-famous museums and a café culture. The city is more than tulips and bicycles with various ideas and art tells a story.

You can start your day with a buttery stroopwafel by the canal, see Van Gogh's masterpieces before noon and enjoy Indonesian rijsttafel for dinner. The city invites you to slow down and feel in its charm whether you’re on foot, by bike, or gliding along the canals. The Dutch capital never loses its cool even in the chillier months when fairy lights reflect off the frozen canals creating a beautiful winter scene.
