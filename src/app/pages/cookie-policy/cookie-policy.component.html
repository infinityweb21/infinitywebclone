Let’s talk cookies! Not the chocolate chip kind, but the little files that help our website run smoother and smarter. At Infinityfarecompare, we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and make sure things work the way they should. Nothing shady, just tech that helps us help you.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files or data stored on your device when you visit any website. They don’t take up much space, don’t collect personal data directly, and definitely don’t bite. Their job? To remember your preferences, keep the site running efficiently, and offer a more personalized experience.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember who you are when you return
  • Improve site speed and functionality
  • Track which pages you like (so we can make them even better)
  • Understand how visitors use the site

It’s all about helping you get where you need to go online and seamless.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies – These are must-haves for basic site features (like page navigation).
  • Performance Cookies – Help us figure out what’s working (and what’s not).
  • Preference Cookies – Remember your settings so you don’t have to redo them every visit.
Controlling Cookies

Not into cookies? That’s cool. You can manage or disable them in your browser settings anytime. Just keep in mind, turning some off might make parts of the site act a little funky.
