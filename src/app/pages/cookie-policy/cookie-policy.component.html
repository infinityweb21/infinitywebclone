Cookie Talk – What You Need to Know

Ever wonder why our website seems to “get you”? Thank cookies for that, not the chocolate chip ones (though we’re fans), but the clever little files that help us personalize your journey on The infinity travel. This Cookie Policy covers what cookies are, how they work for you, and how to control them. It’s simple, useful, and 100% crumb-free, unless you brought your own!

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that get stored on your browser or device when you visit a website. They’re like silent assistants, making sure your next visit feels faster, easier, and just the way you like it. Think of them as your digital travel assistant, they help our site remember things like your login, your search filters, and even your language settings.

We cannot access your device or private files thanks to cookies. They’re more like memory boosts for our site, helping it performs better for you.

The Real Reason Cookies Run the Show

We use cookies to improve your overall experience. Here’s how they help:

  • Maintain your login credentials during your session.
  • Remember what you searched or booked last time
  • Speed up how quickly pages load
  • Personalize travel recommendations and offers
  • Understand what users love (and what they skip)
  • Display pertinent advertisements that don't seem arbitrary or bothersome

Bottom line? Cookies help us give you a smoother, more customized experience on every visit.

What’s in Our Cookie Jar?

Here’s a breakdown of the different cookie categories we use on our platform:

1. Essential Cookies

The main functions of the website depend on these cookies. Without them, you wouldn’t be able to log in, complete bookings, or navigate between pages properly. These are always on.

2. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies keep track of your preferences, such as your language, area, or previous search history. They reduce repetition and give your experience a more unique vibe.

3. Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies gather information about users' interactions with the website in an anonymous manner. We use tools like Google Analytics to see what’s working, what’s not, and how we can improve. No personal data is attached, just helpful trends.

4. Marketing & Advertising Cookies

We want our ads to be useful, not irritating. These cookies help show you promotions and deals that match your travel interests. They may also be used by third-party partners for remarketing and tracking campaign performance.

What About Third-Party Cookies?

Some cookies on our site are placed by trusted third-party services, like ad platforms, analytics tools, or support chat providers. These cookies help enhance your experience or provide insights for better performance.

They have their own cookie policies, which you might want to check out for extra peace of mind.

Customizing the Cookie Experience

Your browser gives you the power to control your cookies. You can:

  • Accept or decline all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Specify preferences for various cookie varieties
  • Get notified when a cookie is being placed

Keep in mind, disabling cookies, especially essential ones, might affect how our site functions. Some features (like remembering your login or keeping items in your cart) may not work properly.

Each browser has its own settings, so we recommend checking your browser’s Help section for step-by-step instructions.

Your Call, Your Cookie Rules

By continuing to browse or use The infinity travel's website, you're agreeing to our use of cookies as described above. If you decide to disable them later, you’re free to do so anytime.
